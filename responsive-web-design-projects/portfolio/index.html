About Jeffery L. Graham

Hello everybody. I’m Jeffery L. Graham, a web developer. I’ve always liked building stuff. Ever since I was little kid with Legos, building anything my mind could come up with. I’ve also always liked putting together puzzles. I remember putting puzzles under the bed in the room I shared with my 2 brothers. That was the only space in our small apartment I was sure that the puzzle wouldn’t get messed with.

Now, as an adult, I still like building things and solving puzzles. But now instead of bricks & puzzle pieces, I use code & programming. That transition started 20 years ago when I was building websites on Angelfire in my dorm room on Friday nights when most of my friends were out drinking. And even though I ended up going into education after college, my affinity for coding and building stuff never died. From free sites, like the Odin Project & freeCodeCamp, to paid sites, like from Udemy, Udacity, Treehouse, I’ve continued to learn how to build stuff on the web, but most importantly, how to do it properly.

Now, let put all my knowledge & experience into building cool stuff for you.
